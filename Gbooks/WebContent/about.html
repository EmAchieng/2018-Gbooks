About Us

Who Are We?

We are students in Gachon who have been wondering what to do with the many pile of books we own with limited space. We have created this book platform to sell and distribute books at a cheaper price. This is a great chance to share your books and meet amazing people.

Do you want to share your books? Do you want to clear your bookshelf? You can save money by sharing and exchanging used books on this platform. The books are as good as new. We also check for any defects before putting them on sale. Get the cheapest book from us.
